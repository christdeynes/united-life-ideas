How To Change a Flat Tire

  1. Secure the vehicle so that it will not roll; and roll the spare tire to the scene of the action.
  2. Pry off the wheel cover.
  3. Loosen the lug nuts using a tire iron.
  4. Jack up the vehicle about six inches off the ground.
  5. Remove the flat tire.
  6. Lift the spare onto the lug bolts.
  7. Replace the lugs nuts and tighten them by hand.
  8. Lower the vehicle and tighten the lug nuts with the tire iron.
  9. Replace the wheel cover of hub cap.
