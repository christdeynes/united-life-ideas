How To Do Laundry

  1. Separate your clothes into light colors, dark colors, whites, and blacks.
  2. Load one set of clothes (like light colors) into the washing machine.
  3. Load your soap and fabric softener into the appropriate slots.
  4. Close the lid and select the appropriate setting for the clothes you're washing.
  5. Turn the fabric softener option on.
  6. Press start and wait.
